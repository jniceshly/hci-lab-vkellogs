History

Founded in 1906 by Will Keith Kellogg in Battle Creek, Michigan, VKellogg’s has been a pioneer in the breakfast foods and snacks industry for over a century. From its humble beginnings, VKellogg’s has grown into a globally recognized brand, renowned for its commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. As one of the world's leading cereal manufacturers, VKellogg's has consistently introduced new and exciting products, making breakfast a delightful and nutritious experience for millions of families worldwide.

Mission

Our mission at VKellogg’s is to enhance the lives of our customers by providing delicious, high-quality breakfast foods and snacks that inspire healthy lifestyles. We are dedicated to fostering a user-friendly online experience that reflects our rich heritage and commitment to excellence. Through our new website, we aim to connect with our global audience, offering easy access to our diverse range of products and recipes, and elevating customer service to new heights. At VKellogg’s, we believe that every day should start with a smile, and we are here to make that possible for everyone, everywhere.
